ABOUT US

#SWOLEDENTISTS does things a little differently. Founded by a black belt martial artist and student doctor, we're working towards a more proactive approach to health in our clients.

With a focus on other healthcare providers, we're aiming to provide curriculum that is time efficient, flexible, and adjusted for the unique needs of these clients. However, this is not limited to just these clients, as we bring this same expertise to all...

After all, it's just what the doctor ordered.
